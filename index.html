About Us

Experience world class online stock market brokerage service in Bangladesh. ​Midway Securities Ltd. is one of the oldest, most prominent and well recognized Securities Brokerage house in the Dhaka Stock Exchange, providing client service with success since 1975. In recent times our company has grown into a promising name for innovative and multidimensional brokerage services, pioneering the age of full fledged online share trading. Helping you invest your money is a job we take very seriously. ​
